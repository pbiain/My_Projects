## 1. Product Overview  


### What We Offer  


> MoveAtlas is a digital membership platform that provides access to 1000+ sports studios across 15+ worldwide cities through one subscription. It eliminates the friction of studio-hopping and allows members to explore diverse fitness activities without long-term commitments.


### Product Category  


- **Industry:** Lifestyle -and Fitness / SaaS Marketplace  
- **Type:** Membership Platform  
- **Model:** Subscription  


---


## 2. Core Features & Benefits  


### Feature 1: Unified Membership  


- **What it does:** Provides one subscription across multiple venues, countries and cities  
- **Why it matters:** Total flexibility and convenience  
- **Key differentiator:** Cross-city -and country access, not limited to a single studio  


### Feature 2: Real-Time Reservation - and Booking App  


- **What it does:** Enables instant class booking and schedule management  
- **Why it matters:** Saves time and reduces friction  
- **Key differentiator:** Seamless UX built for scalability  


### Feature 3: Partner Dashboard  


- **What it does:** Gives studios real-time booking and occupancy insights  
- **Why it matters:** Improves revenue predictability  
- **Key differentiator:** Designed for community growth, not extraction  


### Feature 4: Scalable APIs  


- **What it does:** Enables system integrations across world-wide markets  
- **Why it matters:** Reliability and expansion capability  
- **Key differentiator:** Built for cross-border scalability

## 3. Use Cases  


### Use Case 1: Explorer Member  


- **Who:** Urban professional  
- **Situation:** Tired of single-gym routine  
- **How They Use It:** Books different sports throughout the week  
- **Outcome:** Higher engagement and fitness consistency  


### Use Case 2: Traveling Professional  


- **Who:** Consultant traveling across cities  
- **Situation:** Needs workouts in multiple countries  
- **How They Use It:** Uses same membership in different cities  
- **Outcome:** Seamless continuity  


### Use Case 3: Studio Owner  


- **Who:** Boutique fitness operator  
- **Situation:** Empty mid-week classes  
- **How They Use It:** Lists classes on MoveAtlas platform  
- **Outcome:** Increased occupancy and stable revenue
