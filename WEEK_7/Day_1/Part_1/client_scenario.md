# Client Scenario

## Option C: Retail Customer Service Chatbot

**Client Goal:** A retailer wants to deploy a customer service chatbot to handle common customer inquiries including returns, product questions, and complaints.

**Key Requirements:**
- Must provide helpful and accurate responses
- Should maintain a friendly, professional tone
- Must effectively resolve or escalate customer issues

**Main Concerns/Failure Modes:**
- Unhelpful or incorrect information
- Inappropriate tone (too casual, too robotic, or rude)
- Poor resolution rate (not solving problems)
- Inability to handle edge cases or escalate properly
